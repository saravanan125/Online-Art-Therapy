About This Session

This 60-minute session is fully guided by a certified art therapist. We focus on "Process over Product," meaning the goal is not to create a beautiful painting, but to use colors and brushstrokes to externalize feelings that are hard to put into words.

You will be guided through a series of prompts designed to release tension, identify underlying stressors, and promote a sense of calm.

What You'll Learn

  • Emotional Identification

    Learn to associate colors with specific emotional states.

  • Stress Reduction Techniques

    Breathing exercises integrated with brush movement.

  • Self-Validation

    Building confidence in your own narrative and expression.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be good at art?

Not at all! This therapy is suitable for absolute beginners. We focus on expression, not skill.

What materials do I need?

Basic watercolors or acrylics, paper, and a few brushes. A list will be emailed upon booking.
